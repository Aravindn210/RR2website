AUTO EXPO PAVILION

Exhibition Design Excellence

Project Overview

An award-winning pavilion designed for a luxury automotive brand, focusing on speed and aerodynamic aesthetics.

The Auto Expo Pavilion project challenged RR2Global to create a structure that felt fast even when standing still. We achieved this through fluid architectural lines, metallic finishes, and integrated LED tracks that mimicked light trails.

The pavilion featured immersive VR driving simulators and a central rotating platform to showcase the flagship vehicle. Our strategic spatial planning ensured a smooth visitor flow during peak expo hours, managing thousands of visitors daily with ease.

Project Brief

  • Client Velocita Motors
  • Category Exhibition Design
  • Location Abu Dhabi, UAE
  • Completion November 2024
