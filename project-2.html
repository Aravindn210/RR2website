MODERN OFFICE HUB

Interior Design Innovation

Project Overview

Redefining the modern workspace for a leading digital agency, focusing on collaboration and biophilic design.

The Modern Office Hub project involved transforming a 5,000 sq ft shell into a high-performance workspace. RR2Global focused on open-plan layouts that encourage spontaneous interaction while providing acoustic privacy in designated deep-work areas.

Using sustainable materials and smart lighting systems, we created an environment that boosts productivity and employee wellbeing. Integration of lush greenery and natural textures brings a sense of calm to the high-energy digital work environment.

Project Brief

  • Client Nexus Digital
  • Category Interior Design
  • Location Riyadh, KSA
  • Completion December 2024
