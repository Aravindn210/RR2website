GLOBAL TRADE FAIR

Exhibition Management Excellence

Project Overview

Managing the infrastructure and logistical flow for one of the largest multi-industry trade fairs in the region.

The Global Trade Fair project was a massive undertaking that required the coordination of over 200 exhibitors across 10 halls. RR2Global provided the full technical infrastructure, including power distribution, lighting grids, and standard shell schemes for smaller participants.

We also designed and executed the main entrance gates and the central networking pavilion, which served as the social hub for international delegates. Our management team ensured that move-in and move-out operations were handled with zero delays, a critical factor for an event of this scale.

Project Brief

  • Client Trade & Commerce Bureau
  • Category Exhibition Management
  • Location Frankfurt, Germany
  • Completion May 2024
