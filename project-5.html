CORPORATE HQ FIT-OUT

Interior Architecture Excellence

Project Overview

A full-scale architectural fit-out for a multinational financial institution, emphasizing transparency and structural power.

The Corporate HQ Fit-out project required a balance of formal corporate standards and modern, open architectural principles. RR2Global utilized premium materials like Italian marble, architectural glass, and brushed brass to create a headquarters that exudes strength and reliability.

The layout includes modular boardrooms, executive lounges, and a central social atrium that connects all three floors. Every element, from the custom furniture to the acoustic ceiling panels, was designed to create a silent, powerful working environment.

Project Brief

  • Client Heritage Bank
  • Category Interior Architecture
  • Location London, UK
  • Completion June 2024
